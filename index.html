Planet Overview

  • Uranus
    • Discovered in 1781, orignally thought to be a comet or star.
    • First planet to be discovered by telescope.
    • Uranus is consider anice giant.
  • Earth
    • Only known planet with life.
    • Earth isn't actually a round planet.
    • Earth's moon is slowly moving 4cm away from it every year.
  • Mars
    • Mars is half the size of Earth.
    • Mars is 50% further away from the sun than Earth is.
    • Mars was named after the ancient Roman god of War due to its color.
  • Saturn
    • Saturn is the second largest planet in our solar system.
    • It takes 29 earth years for Saturn to orbit the sun.
    • It would take 9 Earths side by side to span Saturn's diameter.
